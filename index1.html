About us

Saraswati Modern School

Saraswati Modern Sr. Sec. School is a recognized co-educational school affiliated to CBSE upto class XII with Science (Non – Medical), Arts and Commerce streams. The school focuses on three basic values: Light, Learn and Lead. Hence every effort is directed towards building these values leading to overall responsible dynamic personality development among students. The school is spread over an area of 3.7 acres and is located in Pratap Singh Wala, Hambran Road. It is managed by Mrs. Kamla Jain Charitable Trust a board of eminent educationists and futurists.
