Admission Procedure

Please visit us with prior appointment b/w 9:00 am to 3:00 pm any day from Monday to Friday and on Saturday b/w 9:00 am to 11:30 am. The admission office will give detailed information about the school, methodology, facilities,fees structure and guide you with the admission procedure followed by a capus tour.
